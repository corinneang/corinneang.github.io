CORINNE ANG

type design +

CORINNE (with one R and two Ns) is a Filipino graphic and type designer currently based in Brooklyn, NY. She works with a range of projects including (but not limited to) publications, typefaces & lettering, brand identities and web design.

Her personal practice investigates graphic form as a shared space for communities, memories and multiplicity. In her free time she explores typographic form at asddf.
